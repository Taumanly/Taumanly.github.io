Choose Your Poison

Welcome to Dr. Pryor's and Dr. Lemus's labratory . You have entered at the wrong time! A poisonous flask has dropped on the floor contaminating the entire Lab. Its up to you to find as many Antidote Serums in order to survive. Good Luck!

Game Rules

  • Choose a random square to reveal whats underneath
  • Revealing a skull, ends the game
  • Revealing a Antidote syringe, will extend your life and win the game
